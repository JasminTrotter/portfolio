About Me

I fell in love with web development when I discovered how much I enjoy building applications to manage my ballet instruction business. Coding is like a puzzle. When I put the pieces together correctly and it works I get a rush. I strive for cleanliness and simplicity and in my UX/UI and code, which improve with new project I create. A developer can always grow and perfect her craft. For that reason, I am a lifelong learner. When I'm not coding, I'm taking or teaching a dance class, travelling to explore a new city, or I'm at home eating doughnuts while surfing the web.
